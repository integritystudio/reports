## Venue Changes for 2026

The **Austin Convention Center is undergoing redevelopment and expansion** during SXSW 2026. Instead, the festival features an expanded footprint across Downtown Austin with:

- **Three Clubhouses** spread throughout downtown, serving as hubs and home bases for each of the three main festivals (Conference, Film & TV, Music)
- More opportunities to explore Austin's neighborhoods, unique venues, and local culture
