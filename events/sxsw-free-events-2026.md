## Tips for Badge-Free SXSW

- **RSVP early** - Free events fill up fast; RSVP as soon as they're announced
- **Check [RSVPATX.com](https://rsvpatx.com/rsvp-list)** for a comprehensive list of RSVP-able events and parties
- **SXSW "Houses"** are branded pop-up spaces that are often free with RSVP
- **Arrive early** - Many free events have capacity limits
- Some events offer **priority access for badgeholders** even when open to the public
