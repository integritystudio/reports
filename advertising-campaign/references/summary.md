# Advertising Campaign Skill Reference

## Overview
Comprehensive advertising campaign generator that builds complete promotional strategies from target audience analysis through execution planning.

## Core Framework

**Input Dimensions:**
- Product/service characteristics and USPs
- Target audience specifications (demo, psycho, behavioral)
- Business goals (awareness, trial, loyalty, revenue)
- Budget constraints (optional, generates allocation if provided)
- Timeline/launch requirements

**Output Structure:**
```
1. Audience Insights
   ├── Demographic profile
   ├── Psychographic profile
   ├── Media consumption habits
   └── Purchase drivers

2. Strategic Positioning
   ├── Brand positioning statement
   ├── Unique value proposition
   ├── Competitive differentiation
   └── Tone & voice guidelines

3. Creative Strategy
   ├── Key messages (primary + secondary)
   ├── Slogans (2-3 options with reasoning)
   ├── Visual identity guidance
   └── Content themes

4. Media Strategy
   ├── Channel selection (primary, secondary, tertiary)
   ├── Platform-specific tactics
   ├── Frequency & reach targets
   ├── Budget allocation %
   └── Timeline/seasonality

5. Activation & Partnerships
   ├── Launch strategy
   ├── Influencer/ambassador approach
   ├── Experiential activities
   ├── Sampling/trial programs
   └── Strategic partnerships

6. Measurement & Optimization
   ├── KPIs per channel
   ├── Tracking mechanisms
   ├── Attribution model
   └── Optimization triggers
```

## Key Capabilities

### Audience Segmentation
- Demographic targeting (age, income, education, family status)
- Psychographic profiling (values, lifestyle, aspirations)
- Behavioral analysis (media habits, shopping patterns, device usage)
- Geographic and seasonal considerations

### Message Development
- Positioning against competitive set
- Emotional and rational benefit articulation
- Tone customization (premium, casual, educational, humorous, etc.)
- Message hierarchy and consistency frameworks

### Channel Strategy
- **Digital**: Social (Meta, TikTok, LinkedIn, Pinterest), search, display, email, programmatic
- **Traditional**: TV, radio, print, outdoor (OOH)
- **Emerging**: Podcasts, streaming platforms, gaming, metaverse
- **Owned**: Email lists, website, community platforms, loyalty programs

### Budget Optimization
- Recommended allocation across channels
- Cost-per-acquisition benchmarks
- ROI projections by channel
- Contingency reserve planning

### Campaign Mechanics
- Multi-phase rollout strategies
- Influencer selection criteria
- Event activation concepts
- Sampling and trial mechanics
- PR and earned media hooks
- Partnership sourcing strategies

## Variables Handled

- **Product Stage**: Launch, relaunch, mature, declining
- **Budget Scale**: Startup (~$10K-50K), SMB ($50K-500K), Enterprise ($500K+)
- **Category Dynamics**: Luxury vs. commodity, B2B vs. B2C, services vs. goods
- **Cultural Context**: Regional, seasonal, trend-based, occasion-driven
- **Competitive Intensity**: Blue ocean vs. crowded category
- **Measurement Sophistication**: Basic tracking through attribution modeling
