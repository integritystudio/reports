# Advertising Campaign Generator

Create data-driven advertising campaigns from concept to execution strategy.

## Usage

```
/advertising-campaign [product/service] [target audience] [goals]
```

Or naturally reference campaign needs:
- "create an advertising campaign for a new energy drink targeting young adults aged 18-30"
- "help me develop a marketing campaign for eco-friendly packaging"
- "generate an ad campaign strategy for a fitness app targeting busy professionals"

## What You Get

- **Target Audience Profile** — Demographics, psychographics, behaviors, pain points
- **Key Messages & Slogans** — Brand voice, core positioning, memorable taglines
- **Media Channel Strategy** — Digital, traditional, social, and emerging platforms
- **Content Calendar** — Campaign timeline and milestones
- **Promotional Activities** — Events, partnerships, influencer collaborations, sampling
- **Success Metrics** — KPIs and measurement framework
- **Budget Allocation** — Recommended spend distribution across channels

## Campaign Dimensions

### Audience Analysis
- Age, gender, income, education, lifestyle
- Values, aspirations, pain points
- Media consumption habits
- Purchase decision drivers

### Messaging Framework
- Brand positioning statement
- Unique value proposition
- Tone and voice guidelines
- Primary and secondary messages
- 2-3 memorable slogans

### Media Strategy
- Primary channels (social, digital, TV, print, OOH, radio)
- Platform-specific content approaches
- Frequency and timing recommendations
- Partnership opportunities

### Activation Plan
- Launch event or PR strategy
- Influencer/ambassador partnerships
- Experiential marketing activities
- Sampling or trial programs
- Cross-promotion strategies

## Examples

**Input**: "Create campaign for sustainable fashion brand targeting eco-conscious millennials"
**Output**:
- Audience: Millennials (25-40), college-educated, urban, values sustainability
- Slogan: "Wear Your Values"
- Channels: Instagram, TikTok, sustainable lifestyle blogs, zero-waste retail pop-ups
- Activities: Influencer partnerships with eco-activists, documentary collaboration, clothing swap events

**Input**: "Advertising campaign for premium coffee subscription service, target remote workers"
**Output**:
- Audience: Professionals 25-50, WFH, premium product interest, productivity-focused
- Message: "Your Office Upgrade"
- Channels: LinkedIn, productivity podcasts, workspace design publications, Slack app integration
- Activities: Free trial partnerships with coworking spaces, webinar series, community building

## Campaign Structure

1. **Research Phase** — Audience and competitive landscape analysis
2. **Strategy Phase** — Positioning, messaging, media selection
3. **Creative Phase** — Slogan development, content concepts
4. **Execution Phase** — Timeline, partnerships, budget allocation
5. **Measurement Phase** — KPIs, tracking mechanisms
