Executive Summary

Edghar Fhernandes and Nadyne Cruz are internationally acclaimed Brazilian Zouk dancers, instructors, and choreographers based in Sao Paulo, Brazil. With a combined 20+ years of dance experience and 10,000+ students taught, they have established themselves as elite-tier artists in the global Brazilian Zouk circuit. Their partnership, formed in 2017, blends Edghar's competitive ballroom championship background and television appearances (Got Talent Brasil, Dancing with the Stars Brasil) with Nadyne's classical ballet foundation and psychology-informed pedagogy.

They have taught in 9+ countries across 4 continents, maintain a recurring position at ZoukMX's prestigious 100-hour program in Playa del Carmen, and are directly connected to Jaime Aroxa -- one of the three co-creators of Brazilian Zouk. Their signature style emphasizes counterbalances, playful head movements, and technical precision delivered through a human-centered teaching approach.

2. Teaching Philosophy & Methodology

Core Methodology Pillars

  • Human-Centered Learning: Nadyne's psychology background creates a learning environment that prioritizes emotional safety, body awareness, and individual expression alongside technical instruction
  • Body Awareness First: Students are guided to explore movement through their own physical capabilities rather than forced into rigid patterns
  • Confidence & Freedom: The pedagogical goal is building students who dance with personal authenticity, not rote reproduction of the instructors' style
  • Cross-Disciplinary Integration: Multi-style expertise allows drawing on ballet technique to inform zouk flow, ballroom frame to improve connection, and hip-hop musicality to enhance expression
  • Technical Precision with Playfulness: Their signature approach balances demanding technical standards with a joyful, accessible teaching energy
Differentiator: While many zouk instructors teach from a purely movement-based perspective, Edghar & Nadyne's integration of psychology and multi-style dance expertise creates a pedagogical approach that is rare in the Brazilian Zouk circuit. This positions them uniquely for wellness-oriented programming and deeper student transformation beyond physical technique.
