Executive Summary

ZoukMX is a premier Brazilian Zouk dance festival and immersive training program held annually in Playa del Carmen, Mexico, now in its 11th year of operation. Organized by Joe Sandoval, the event has evolved from a weekend festival into a month-long experience combining a 100-hour intensive training program (across 7 specialized tracks), pre-festival workshops, a multi-day festival with BZDC-sanctioned Jack & Jill competitions, and unique excursion parties at cenotes and jungle lagoons. The event draws attendees from 25+ countries and features 14+ internationally renowned Brazilian Zouk artists and DJs. ZoukMX operates within the rapidly growing global music festival market (valued at $3.76B in 2025, CAGR 23.17%) and music tourism sector ($96.78B in 2024, CAGR 18.8%), while benefiting from Quintana Roo's position as Mexico's top tourism destination with 24.4M visitors in 2025. With 8,600+ Instagram followers and a strong reputation in the global Zouk community, ZoukMX is well-positioned as the only 100-hour Brazilian Zouk intensive in North America, though opportunities exist to expand digital presence, formalize business registration, and increase capacity to capture the growing demand for experiential dance travel.

1. Company Overview

Mission & Value Proposition

ZoukMX creates a unique experience mixing an ultimate beach vacation and dance festival into one. The event is built around Brazilian Zouk, a partner dance originating from Brazil, and aims to provide world-class training, unforgettable music, and authentic cultural experiences in one of the most beautiful settings in Central America.

Geographic Focus

Based in Playa del Carmen, Quintana Roo, Mexico (Riviera Maya). Attendees fly into Cancun International Airport and take the ADO premier shuttle ($17 one way, every 30 min) to Playa del Carmen. The event draws participants from 25+ countries, with instruction primarily from Brazilian artists.

Key Differentiators

  • Only 100-hour Brazilian Zouk intensive training program in North America
  • Month-long immersive experience (not just a weekend festival)
  • BZDC-sanctioned Jack & Jill competition
  • Unique venue experiences (cenotes, jungle lagoons, rooftops)
  • 7 specialized training tracks with audition-based placement
  • International Brazilian training team
  • 11-year track record in Mexico

11. Conclusions & Recommendations

Key Findings

  1. Market leader in niche: ZoukMX is the only 100-hour Brazilian Zouk intensive in North America and one of only two month-long Zouk experiences globally (alongside Zouk Island in Thailand). This positions it as a premium, differentiated offering.
  2. Strong tailwinds: The event benefits from three high-growth macro trends: music tourism (18.8% CAGR), experiential travel, and wellness retreats ($222.5B market). The 66% of event travel inquiries from ages 24–35 matches ZoukMX's core demographic.
  3. Location advantage: Playa del Carmen within Quintana Roo (Mexico's #1 tourism state at 46.4% of foreign tourism revenue) provides strong infrastructure, international accessibility, and natural appeal (cenotes, beaches, jungle).
  4. Brand maturity: 11 years of operation, 8,600+ Instagram followers, presence on 5+ dance directories, press coverage in The Yucatan Times, and BZDC affiliation establish credibility.
  5. Expansion opportunities: Online presence gaps (no Google Business, TripAdvisor, YouTube, or transparent pricing) limit discoverability outside the existing Zouk community.

Recommendations for Further Research

  • Obtain specific ticket pricing data (requires Eventbrite purchase flow or direct inquiry)
