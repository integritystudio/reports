### Webinar 1: "AI Policy Development for Nonprofits: 60-Minute Crash Course"

**Duration:** 60 minutes (45 min presentation + 15 min Q&A)

**Target Audience:** Executive Directors, CTOs, Board Members, Compliance Officers

**Learning Objectives:**
- Understand why AI policies are essential for nonprofits
- Learn the 8 essential elements of an effective AI policy
- Get a template they can customize immediately
- Understand the approval and rollout process

**Outline:**

**Introduction (5 minutes)**
- Welcome and housekeeping
- Poll: "Does your organization have an AI policy?" (Yes/No/In Development)
- Today's agenda

**Part 1: The AI Policy Gap (10 minutes)**
- Statistics: 85%+ using AI, 65-70% without formal policies, only 28% with formal AI training
- Real-world risks: data breaches, bias incidents, compliance failures
- Why nonprofits are particularly vulnerable
- Case study: What happens when AI goes wrong (brief example)

**Part 2: The 8 Essential Elements (20 minutes)**
1. **Purpose and Scope** - Why this policy exists and what it covers
2. **Roles and Responsibilities** - Who oversees AI governance
3. **Approved Use Cases** - What AI applications are permitted
4. **Prohibited Uses** - Red lines and restrictions
5. **Data Privacy and Security** - How to protect sensitive information
6. **Bias Mitigation** - Ensuring fairness and equity
7. **Human Oversight** - Where humans must stay in the loop
8. **Review and Updates** - How often to revisit the policy

[For each element: 2-3 minutes with examples and template preview]

**Part 3: Implementation Roadmap (7 minutes)**
- Week 1-2: Draft using template
- Week 3-4: Stakeholder review (board, staff, legal)
- Week 5-6: Board approval
- Week 7-8: Staff communication and training
- Ongoing: Monitoring and updates

**Part 4: Common Pitfalls (3 minutes)**
- Making it too long (aim for 3-5 pages)
- Too restrictive (stifles innovation)
- Too permissive (creates risk)
- Set it and forget it (needs regular updates)

**Q&A (15 minutes)**
- Open floor for questions
- Address specific scenarios

**Closing (2 minutes)**
- Recap of key takeaways
- Resources being sent (template, checklist, recording)
- Next webinar announcement
- Free consultation offer
- Thank you

**Slides:**
- 20-25 slides
- Visual, minimal text
- Include template screenshots
- Real examples from nonprofit policies

**Follow-Up:**
- Recording sent within 24 hours
- AI Policy Template (downloadable)
- 30-day implementation checklist
- Survey for feedback and topic requests
- Offer for free consultation
