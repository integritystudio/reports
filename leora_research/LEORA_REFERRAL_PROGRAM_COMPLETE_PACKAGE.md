## ğŸ¯ Bonus Structure Rationale

### Why Tiered Payments?

**Payment 1 (At Hire):**
- 30-40% of total bonus
- Immediate gratitude
- Validates the referrer's judgment
- Encourages continued participation

**Payment 2 (90 Days):**
- 30-35% of total bonus
- Confirms cultural fit
- Ensures past probationary period
- Reduces risk of quick turnover

**Payment 3 (180 Days):**
- 30-35% of total bonus
- Confirms long-term success
- Rewards referrer for quality
- Aligns with retention goals

### Position Tiers Explained

**Tier 1 - CNA ($750):**
- Largest hiring volume
- Entry-level position
- Competitive with market

**Tier 2 - LVN ($1,000):**
- Licensed position
- Higher skill requirement
- Moderate scarcity

**Tier 3 - RN ($1,500):**
- Advanced degree required
- Critical leadership roles
- High market demand

**Tier 4 - Specialized ($2,000):**
- Hard-to-fill positions
- Specialized certifications
- Strategic importance
- Examples: Bilingual RN, wound care specialist, dementia care expert

### How to Use Both Together

**Step 1: Employee Submits Referral**
- Use form or email
- HR logs in tracking system

**Step 2: Initial Screening**
- Use `leora-recruiting-assistant` agent to evaluate resume
- Agent applies Leora Excellence Profile
- Produces recommendation: Interview / Maybe / Pass

**Step 3: Interview Preparation**
- Agent generates customized interview questions
- Prepares scenarios based on position
- Brief team members on cultural fit assessment

**Step 4: Candidate Evaluation**
- Agent analyzes interview responses
- Rates on 4-dimension matrix
- Provides hiring recommendation with evidence

**Step 5: Decision & Feedback**
- Make informed hiring decision
- Provide feedback to referring employee
- Process accordingly in tracking system
