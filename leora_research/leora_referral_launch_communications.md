## FAQ Handout

**Q: Who can I refer?**
A: Anyone with healthcare experience or certifications! Friends, former colleagues, family members, classmates - anyone you think would be a great fit.

**Q: Do I need to tell them I'm referring them?**
A: Yes! They must mention your name in their application for you to get credit.

**Q: How many people can I refer?**
A: Unlimited! Refer as many qualified candidates as you know.

**Q: When do I get paid?**
A: Three installments - when they're hired, after 90 days, and after 180 days (if they're still employed and performing well).

**Q: What if two people refer the same candidate?**
A: First submission gets credit. Submit quickly!

**Q: Can I refer family members?**
A: Yes, as long as there's no direct reporting relationship or conflict of interest.

**Q: What if they don't get hired?**
A: You can refer them again for different positions or wait and resubmit in the future.

**Q: Do I need to be here a certain amount of time?**
A: Yes, at least 60 days of employment.

**Q: What if I leave Leora before all milestones are paid?**
A: You must be actively employed when each bonus is paid to receive it.

**Q: How do I track my referrals?**
A: Visit leorahomehealth.com/my-referrals or contact HR for updates.

## Manager Talking Points

**For team leaders to discuss with their teams:**

1. **Emphasize quality over quantity**: "One great referral is better than five mediocre ones"

2. **Personalize the impact**: "When you refer someone great, YOU benefit by working with them every day"

3. **Address concerns**: "No, you won't get in trouble if your referral doesn't work out - we appreciate you trying"

4. **Share the 'why'**: "Referred employees stay 88% longer than Indeed hires - that means less turnover stress for all of us"

5. **Make it conversational**: "Who's the best caregiver you've ever worked with? Are they available?"

6. **Celebrate publicly**: Recognize referrals in team meetings, even before they're hired

7. **Remove barriers**: "Don't overthink it - if you think they'd be good, submit them and let us evaluate"
