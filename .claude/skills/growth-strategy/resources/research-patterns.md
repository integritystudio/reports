# Growth Strategy Research Patterns

Validated research patterns extracted from the Illumination Holiday Lighting session (2026-02-08). Use as reference for structuring agent prompts and organizing findings.

## Category 1: Growth Opportunities

### Revenue Diversification Research

Search for complementary services in the same industry vertical:
- "[industry] year-round services"
- "[industry] off-season revenue"
- "permanent [product] installation [city]"
- "[industry] landscape lighting / event lighting / patio lighting"
- "[industry] maintenance contracts"

### Target Community Research

Search for growing residential areas:
- "master planned communities [city] [year]"
- "new construction [city] [year]"
- "fastest growing suburbs [city]"
- "HOA communities [city]"
- "[city] new developments"
- "[city] home builders ranked [year]"

Build a table with: Community, Location, Why Target (HOA activity, home values, growth).

### Service Area Expansion

Rank surrounding cities by growth rate. Search:
- "fastest growing cities near [city]"
- "population growth [suburb] TX [year]"
- "[suburb] new home permits [year]"

### Upsell Research

- "smart [product] control [year]"
- "[city] energy rebates LED / lighting"
- "[industry] maintenance contract pricing"
- "[industry] storage services"

## Category 2: Marketing Opportunities

### Digital Presence Audit

Check current presence on:
- Google Business Profile (search Google Maps for company name)
- Yelp (search yelp.com)
- Facebook (search facebook.com)
- Instagram (search instagram.com/[variations])
- Nextdoor (search business.nextdoor.com)
- BBB (search bbb.org)

### Local SEO Keywords

Generate target keywords:
- "[service] [city] TX"
- "[service] installer [city]"
- "commercial [service] [city]"
- "permanent [product] [city]"
- "[service] [neighborhood name]"

### Social Media Platform Comparison

Build a table with: Platform, Why (user demo), Cost, Priority.
Standard ranking for home services in Austin:
1. Nextdoor (77% homeowners)
2. Instagram (visual before/after)
3. Facebook (reviews, groups, zip-code ads)
4. TikTok/YouTube Shorts (time-lapse content)

### Review Generation Strategy

Check current review counts across all platforms. For a 10-year business with <5 total reviews, this is a CRITICAL gap. Recommendations:
- Direct link text/email after job completion
- QR code review cards (physical)
- "$X off next season" referral incentive
- Respond to all reviews within 24 hours

### Local Advertising Research

Search for Austin-area publications and newsletters:
- "Stroll [city]" (neighborhood magazines)
- Neighborhood association newsletters (ZNA, BCNA, etc.)
- "[city] Monthly" magazine
- "[city] Chronicle" / local papers
- "ATXtoday" / local digital newsletters

## Category 3: Partnership Opportunities

### Partnership Categories & Search Patterns

| Category | Search Query | Approach |
|----------|-------------|----------|
| Landscaping | "[city] landscaping companies" | Reciprocal referral, $0 cost |
| Property Mgmt | "[city] property management firms" | Preferred vendor, common area packages |
| HOAs | "[city] HOA management companies" | Volume pricing, community entrance lighting |
| Real Estate | "[city] top real estate agents" | New homeowner consultation gift |
| Event/Wedding | "wedding planners [city]", "AWPA [city]" | Preferred vendor list, free demo |
| Home Builders | "[city] home builders ranked [year]" | Light model homes, welcome package flyers |
| Complementary | "[related service] [city]" | Cross-referral, leave cards |
| Chamber | "[city] chamber of commerce join" | Networking, directory, credibility |

### Partnership Approach Templates

**Landscapers:** "You refer lighting, I refer landscape." Visit 5-10 companies. Leave cards + portfolio. $0 cost.

**Property Managers:** "Common area holiday lighting package for entrances, clubhouses, pools. Volume pricing for 100+ home communities."

**Real Estate:** "New homeowner holiday lighting consultation as a gift from the agent. Referral fee/discount for their clients."

**Event Planners:** Join vendor associations (AWPA, NACE). Contact venue managers. Offer free demo or discounted first job.

**Builders:** Contact sales offices. Offer to light model homes. Provide flyers for new homeowner welcome packages.

## Category 4: Certification & Licensing

### Industry Certification Research

Search for:
- "[industry] certification [year]"
- "[industry] training program [state/region]"
- "[industry] association certification"
- "[industry] trade association"

Key fields to capture: name, what you get, training dates/locations, cost, marketing value.

### Standard Certifications (All Industries)

| Cert | Cost | Priority | Notes |
|------|------|----------|-------|
| OSHA 10-Hour Construction | $45-$59 | High | Online, self-paced, DOL card for life |
| BBB Accreditation | $300-$500/yr | Medium | Trust signal, 50+ demo and commercial |
| HomeAdvisor Elite | Free (5+ reviews) | Medium | Need min 5 reviews at 4.5+ |
| Insurance/Bonding | Varies | High | Market "Fully Insured" on all materials |

- Many low-voltage and seasonal services are exempt

### Franchise vs Independent Analysis

For service businesses, always assess:
- Independent margins: typically 45-60% net
- Franchise margins: typically 35-50% after royalties (5-8%)
- Independents capture ~55% of residential market
- Recommendation: Usually stay independent with 5+ years experience

## Category 6: Industry Trends & Technology

### Trend Research

Search for:
- "[industry] trends [year]"
- "[product] dealer program"
- "smart [product] [year]"
- "[industry] sustainability"
- "[city] [industry] new companies [year]"

### Dealer/Distributor Program Research

When a company could become an authorized dealer:
- Search "[brand] become a dealer" or "[brand] dealer application"
- Build comparison table: dealer cost, ongoing fees, territory, training, quota, app
- Always check territory availability for the company's zip code

### Competitive Threat Assessment

Search "[industry] [city] [recent year]" to find new entrants.
Flag any well-funded startups or franchise expansions as competitive threats.
Note their digital marketing strength vs. the subject company.

## Agent Prompt Best Practices

From session telemetry (67 tool uses, 157K tokens, ~33 min):

1. **Be specific:** Name the company, location, zip code, years in business, known gaps
2. **Provide context:** Include owner name, service types, current online presence status
3. **Request specifics:** "For each opportunity, provide URLs, names, costs, and application details"
4. **Set expectations:** "Priority level (high/medium/low) based on potential ROI"
5. **Cover all 6 categories** in a single prompt to maximize agent efficiency
6. **Run in background:** The agent takes 25-35 minutes; use `run_in_background: true`
