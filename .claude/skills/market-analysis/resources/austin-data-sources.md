## Key Austin Metro Stats (Baseline, as of Feb 2026)

Use these as starting reference points; always verify for current data:

- **MSA Population:** 2.5M+ (25th largest US metro)
- **City of Austin Population:** ~1,032,000
- **Growth Rate:** 1.72% annually (4th fastest large metro)
- **Total Households:** ~949,000
- **Homeownership Rate:** 58.9%
- **Median Household Income:** $97,638
- **HUD Median Family Income:** $133,800
- **Median Home Value:** $435,000
- **Owner-Occupied Homes:** ~559,000
