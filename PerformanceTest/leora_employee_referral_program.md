# Leora Home Health Employee Referral Program
## "Bring Your Best" Referral Initiative

---

## Executive Summary

Based on industry research showing that employee referrals significantly outperform other recruitment sources with better retention rates, Leora Home Health is launching a structured employee referral program to build our exceptional team through the people who know us best - our current staff.

**Program Goals:**
- Reduce recruitment costs and time-to-fill
- Improve quality of hires and cultural fit
- Increase employee engagement and retention
- Lower first-year turnover rate
- Build stronger team connections

---

## Program Overview

### Why Employee Referrals Work

**Research-Backed Benefits:**
- Higher quality candidates who understand the role
- Better cultural fit through employee pre-screening
- Faster hiring process (candidates are pre-vetted)
- Lower turnover rates (referrals have 88% better retention than Indeed hires)
- Higher employee engagement (referring employees become more invested)
- Cost-effective (lower cost-per-hire than job boards)

### Program Philosophy

At Leora, we believe our best ambassadors are our current team members. You understand our patient-first values, our family-like culture, and what it takes to thrive here. When you refer someone, you're not just filling a position - you're strengthening our family and ensuring excellent patient care.

## Referral Bonus Structure

### Tiered Bonus System

**Tier 1: Certified Nursing Assistant (CNA)**
- $750 total bonus
- $250 after referral is hired
- $250 after 90 days of successful employment
- $250 after 180 days of successful employment

**Tier 2: Licensed Vocational Nurse (LVN)**
- $1,000 total bonus
- $300 after referral is hired
- $350 after 90 days of successful employment
- $350 after 180 days of successful employment

**Tier 3: Registered Nurse (RN)**
- $1,500 total bonus
- $500 after referral is hired
- $500 after 90 days of successful employment
- $500 after 180 days of successful employment

**Tier 4: Specialized/Hard-to-Fill Positions**
- $2,000 total bonus
- $600 after referral is hired
- $700 after 90 days of successful employment
- $700 after 180 days of successful employment
- Examples: RN with wound care certification, dementia care specialist, bilingual clinicians

### Why Tiered Payments?

- **Immediate reward:** Shows appreciation for the referral right away
- **90-day milestone:** Ensures the candidate completes orientation and demonstrates fit
- **180-day milestone:** Confirms long-term success and reduces turnover costs

## Eligibility Requirements

### Who Can Refer

**Eligible Employees:**
- All current Leora Home Health employees in good standing
- Must have been employed for at least 60 days
- Must be actively employed when bonuses are paid
- Part-time and full-time employees are eligible

**Not Eligible:**
- Owners and managing partners
- Hiring managers for the specific position
- Employees on performance improvement plans
- HR personnel involved in the hiring decision

### Who Can Be Referred

**Eligible Candidates:**
- Not currently employed by Leora Home Health
- Not a previous employee who left within the last 12 months
- Not a contractor or vendor currently working with Leora
- Meets minimum qualifications for the position
- Passes all standard background checks and screenings

## How the Program Works

### Step 1: Submit a Referral

**Method 1: Email Submission**
Send to: referrals@leorahomehealth.com

Include:
- Your name and employee ID
- Candidate's full name
- Candidate's phone number and email
- Position you're referring them for
- Brief description of why they'd be a great fit (2-3 sentences)
- How you know them (friend, former colleague, family, etc.)

**Method 2: Referral Form**
Fill out the online form at: leorahomehealth.com/referrals
(Login with employee credentials)

**Method 3: In-Person**
Give referral information to HR directly

### Step 2: Candidate Application

- Candidate must mention your name in their application
- Candidate must apply within 30 days of your referral submission
- Candidate goes through standard hiring process
- You'll receive confirmation that we received the referral

### Step 3: Interview Process

- You'll be notified when your referral is being interviewed
- You may be asked to provide additional insights about cultural fit
- Standard interview and assessment process applies
- No guarantee of hire (all candidates must meet qualifications)

### Step 4: Hiring Decision

- If hired, you'll receive notification and first bonus payment
- If not selected, you'll receive feedback on why (if possible)
- You can refer the same person for different positions in the future

### Step 5: Track Milestones

- 90-day milestone: Second bonus payment (if referral is still employed and performing well)
- 180-day milestone: Final bonus payment (if referral is still employed and performing well)
- You'll receive reminders before each milestone

## Important Terms & Conditions

### General Rules

1. **No Self-Referrals:** You cannot refer yourself
2. **One Referrer Per Candidate:** First person to refer gets credit
3. **Active Employment Required:** You must be employed when bonuses are paid
4. **Good Standing:** Both you and referral must be in good standing
5. **Standard Process:** Referrals go through normal hiring procedures
6. **Tax Implications:** Bonuses are taxable income and reported on W-2
7. **Program Changes:** Leora reserves right to modify program with notice
8. **No Guarantee:** Referral doesn't guarantee interview or hire

### Disqualification Scenarios

Bonuses will NOT be paid if:
- Referral is terminated for cause within the payment period
- Referral voluntarily resigns before milestone dates
- Referral fails probationary period or performance reviews
- Referring employee is terminated or resigns before payment
- Fraud or misrepresentation is discovered
- Referral was already in our applicant database before referral submission

### Multiple Referrals

- **No limit** on number of people you can refer
- **Unlimited earning potential** (refer 10 CNAs = up to $7,500)
- Each referral tracked independently
- Build your own "referral team" within Leora

## Referral Success Tips

### Who Makes a Great Leora Employee?

Before referring someone, ask yourself:

**The Empathy Test:**
- Have I seen them show genuine compassion to others?
- Do they have patience in difficult situations?
- Are they good listeners who truly care?

**The Reliability Test:**
- Are they punctual and dependable?
- Do they follow through on commitments?
- Can patients and families count on them?

**The Culture Test:**
- Would they embrace our patient-first values?
- Do they work well in team environments?
- Are they comfortable with nurse-led leadership?
- Do they seek long-term employment?

**The Technical Test:**
- Do they have required certifications/licenses?
- Do they have relevant healthcare experience?
- Are they comfortable with technology/documentation?
- Can they handle the physical demands?

### Red Flags to Avoid

Don't refer someone if they:
- Have a history of frequent job changes
- Show lack of empathy or compassion
- Have reliability or attendance issues
- Are uncomfortable with background checks
- Don't align with our values
- Are just "looking for any job"

### How to Approach Potential Referrals

**Good Approach:**
"I work at an amazing home health care company called Leora. We're nurse-owned and have a really supportive, family-like culture. We're looking for compassionate caregivers who put patients first. Based on what I know about you, I think you'd be a great fit. Would you be interested in learning more?"

**Topics to Share:**
- Nurse-owned leadership
- Patient-first mission
- Team support and culture
- Professional development opportunities
- Work-life balance
- Competitive compensation
- Growing industry with career paths

### Pre-Screen Your Referral

Before submitting, have a conversation:
- Do they have the required certifications?
- Are they available for the schedule/hours?
- What's their salary expectation?
- Why are they looking for a new position?
- What matters most to them in a job?
- Can they pass a background check?

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I refer someone who doesn't have their CNA yet?**
A: Yes! If they're currently enrolled in a CNA program, they can apply and be hired pending certification. We'll work with their timeline.

**Q: What if my referral applies for a different position than I suggested?**
A: You still get credit! As long as they mention you in their application and get hired, you're eligible.

**Q: Can I refer a family member?**
A: Yes, as long as they meet qualifications and there's no direct reporting relationship or conflict of interest.

**Q: What if someone else refers the same person?**
A: First submission wins. That's why it's important to submit as soon as you identify someone great!

**Q: Do I need to tell the person I'm referring them?**
A: Yes! They need to mention your name in their application, so communication is important.

**Q: What if my referral gets hired but I leave Leora before the milestones?**
A: You must be actively employed at Leora when each bonus payment is made to receive it.

**Q: Can I refer someone who worked here before?**
A: Only if they left more than 12 months ago and left in good standing.

**Q: What happens if my referral is great but there's no current opening?**
A: We'll keep them in our talent pipeline and reach out when a position opens. You'll still get credit if hired within 6 months of your referral.

**Q: Is there a limit to how many people I can refer?**
A: No limit! Refer as many qualified candidates as you'd like.

**Q: How long does it take to receive bonus payments?**
A: Payments are processed within 2 weeks of each milestone date and included in regular payroll.
