## Executive Summary

The Balloon Collective is a luxury balloon design and installation company based in Austin, Texas, co-founded by Nate "the Great" Culpepper (18+ years experience) and Stacy "Pineapple" Wheeler. Positioned as a nationally-renowned, high-end balloon artistry firm with international travel capability, the company differentiates itself through large-scale sculptural installations, immersive art projects (Hopscotch, Austin Maker Faire, Austin Pride Parade), and convention-level balloon expertise. The Austin metro balloon decor market is highly competitive with 10+ active companies, pricing from $18-$45 per linear foot for garlands. The Balloon Collective occupies the premium tier with a $500 minimum spend and luxury positioning. The broader decorative balloon market is projected to grow from $3.29B (2024) to $4.5B by 2032 at a 4.01% CAGR, while Austin's MSA population of 2.55M and median household income of $99,897 create a strong addressable market for event decor services.

## 1. Company Overview

The Balloon Collective is a luxury balloon design and installation company based in Austin, Texas, with national and international service availability. The company describes itself as a "nationally-renowned team of balloon artists with more than 18 years of experience in the high-end balloon decorating and entertainment industry."

### Services

- **Large-scale sculptural installations** - custom builds for events, corporate activations, immersive experiences
- **Balloon arches** - standard and organic styles
- **Balloon garlands** - pick-up from South Austin studio (Mon-Fri, 8AM-8PM)
- **Balloon twisting entertainment** - live event entertainment
- **Private party decor** - birthdays, weddings, corporate events
- **Balloon deliveries** - including contact-free yard art
- **Couture balloon costumes** - balloon dresses and wearable art

### Notable Projects

- Human-sized balloon labyrinth in **China**
- **The Balloon Wedding** in Australia
- **Molly Balloons** 2017 and 2018 Fashion Show
- **Hopscotch** immersive art experience - "Thunder & Lightning" installation
- **Austin Maker Faire** participation
- **Austin Pride Parade** builds
- **ABC Kite Festival** at Zilker Park - custom rainbow-and-clouds arch (275 balloons, 6 hours)
