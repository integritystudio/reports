Executive Summary

Post Oak Group is a newly launched Houston-based middle-market investment bank, founded December 19, 2025. The firm offers four integrated service lines — M&A Advisory, Capital Markets, Wealth Advisory, and Institutional Research — targeting companies with $10M–$500M in revenue across 26 industry sectors. Led by four Managing Partners (Anthony Treistman, Alexander Treistman, Scott Schopen, and David Chua), the firm claims 300+ professionals across 12 countries with a combined $82.2 billion in historical transaction experience. Post Oak Group is headquartered at 2800 Post Oak Blvd., Houston, TX 77056 and competes across the Texas middle market. As a firm less than four months old at the time of this research, no independent third-party reviews, BBB listing, or FINRA broker-dealer registration could be confirmed through public searches. The middle-market investment banking sector is experiencing strong tailwinds, with global M&A deal volume reaching ~$3.5 trillion in 2025 and over $1 trillion in private equity dry powder targeting the segment.

11. Conclusions and Recommendations

Key Findings

  1. Very new firm: Post Oak Group launched December 19, 2025 — less than four months old. All transaction history ($82.2B) represents team members’ prior-firm experience, not Post Oak Group’s own deal record.
  2. Ambitious positioning: The firm claims 300+ professionals across 12 countries with an integrated four-service-line platform — an unusually large footprint for a firm this new. LinkedIn data suggests a smaller actual team (81–200).
  3. Regulatory gaps: No FINRA broker-dealer registration or SEC investment adviser registration was found. For a firm conducting M&A advisory and private placements, this is a significant finding that warrants direct verification.
  4. Houston-based, not Austin: Despite claiming Texas-wide coverage, Post Oak Group has no confirmed Austin office or presence. Competitors like Westlake Securities, Navidar, and Focus Strategies have deep, multi-decade Austin roots.
  5. Favorable market conditions: The middle-market IB sector is experiencing strong tailwinds — $3.5T in global M&A volume, $1T+ in PE dry powder, and 7.12% CAGR for SME clients.

Recommendations for Further Research

  • FINRA BrokerCheck: Directly search brokercheck.finra.org for “Post Oak Group” and key individual names
  • SEC IAPD: Search adviserinfo.sec.gov for firm and individual registrations
  • TX Secretary of State: Run SOSDirect search ($1) for “Post Oak Group” and “The Post Oak Group”
  • TX Securities Board: Verify dealer/adviser registration via TSSB
  • Individual backgrounds: Verify Managing Partners’ claimed transaction histories via LinkedIn, prior firm deal tombstones, or FINRA U4 filings
  • Client references: Request verifiable client testimonials or deal tombstones from Post Oak Group directly
  • Physical presence: Confirm occupancy at 2800 Post Oak Blvd via Harris County Appraisal District or building directory
