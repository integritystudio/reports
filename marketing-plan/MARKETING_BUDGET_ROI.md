## Cost Per Acquisition (CPA) Analysis

### Lead Generation Costs by Channel

**Webinars:**
- Cost per webinar: $100-$300 (promotion)
- Average attendance: 50-100
- Cost per attendee: $1-$6
- Attendees who become MQL: 20% (10-20)
- Cost per MQL: $5-$30
- MQL to client close rate: 10%
- **Cost per client: $50-$300**

**LinkedIn Ads:**
- Monthly ad spend: $500-$1,000
- Monthly leads: 30-50
- Cost per lead: $10-$33
- Lead to MQL rate: 15% (5-8)
- Cost per MQL: $67-$200
- MQL to client close rate: 8%
- **Cost per client: $835-$2,500**

**Organic Content/SEO:**
- Monthly time investment: 40-60 hours
- Value of time (at $100/hr): $4,000-$6,000
- OR outsourced: $1,000-$2,000/month
- Monthly leads: 20-40
- Cost per lead: $50-$300
- Lead to MQL rate: 30% (6-12)
- Cost per MQL: $167-$1,000
- MQL to client close rate: 12%
- **Cost per client: $1,400-$8,333**

**Referrals/Partnerships:**
- Monthly investment: 10-15 hours relationship building
- Value of time: $1,000-$1,500
- Monthly referrals: 5-10
- Cost per referral: $100-$300
- Referral to MQL rate: 60% (3-6)
- Cost per MQL: $167-$500
- MQL to client close rate: 20%
- **Cost per client: $835-$2,500**

**Blended Average CPA:** $1,000-$3,000 per client

## Optimization Opportunities

### High-ROI Activities to Double Down On

Based on typical nonprofit consulting metrics:

**1. Webinars (Highest ROI)**
- Low cost ($100-300 per event)
- High lead volume (50-100 per event)
- Strong conversion (20% to MQL, 10% MQL to client)
- **Recommendation:** Increase frequency from monthly to bi-weekly in Q3-Q4

**2. Referral Partnerships (Highest Quality Leads)**
- Moderate cost (time investment)
- Lower volume but very high quality
- Excellent conversion (60% to MQL, 20% to client)
- **Recommendation:** Formalize 5-10 partnerships by mid-year

**3. Thought Leadership Content (Long-term Compounding)**
- Medium upfront cost (time or $1K-2K/piece)
- Compounds over time (SEO value grows)
- Builds authority that supports all other channels
- **Recommendation:** Maintain 1-2 posts/week consistently

### Lower-ROI Activities to Minimize

**1. Broad LinkedIn Ads (without optimization)**
- Can be expensive per lead ($10-33)
- Lower conversion if not well-targeted
- **Recommendation:** Start small, optimize aggressively, scale only what converts

**2. Conference Attendance (without speaking)**
- High cost (travel, registration: $1,500-5,000)
- Hard to measure ROI
- **Recommendation:** Only attend if speaking or sponsoring (justified by visibility)
