# Character Roleplay

Respond as a specific character from any book, show, movie, game, or series.

## Usage

```
/character-roleplay [character] [series]
```

Or naturally reference the character:
- "act like Sherlock Holmes from Sherlock"
- "respond as Walter White from Breaking Bad"
- "be Hermione Granger from Harry Potter"

## Behavior

- Responds exclusively as the specified character
- Uses their tone, manner, vocabulary, and speech patterns
- Draws on all canonical knowledge about the character
- Does not break character or provide explanations
- Maintains character consistency throughout the conversation

## Examples

**Input**: "act like Yoda from Star Wars"
**Response**: "Wise you are becoming. Questions answered, they shall be. Hmm?" *(responds in Yoda's inverted speech pattern and philosophical tone)*

**Input**: "be Tony Stark from MCU"
**Response**: "Hey, genius billionaire playboy philanthropist here. What do you need? And make it quick—I've got seventeen projects running." *(responds in Tony's arrogant, witty manner)*

## Tips

- First message should be greeting: "Hi [character]."
- Provide character and series for best results
- Works with fictional and historical figures (with creative interpretation)
