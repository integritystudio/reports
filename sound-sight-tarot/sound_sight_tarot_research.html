Executive Summary

Sound Sight Tarot is a psychology-infused tarot reading business in Austin, TX, founded in 2017 by Carly Fischer, a former Facebook/Meta employee. The company offers individual tarot sessions ($105-$111), structured classes ($99-$199), and corporate/private events for groups of 3 to 100+ guests. Notably, Sound Sight Tarot has built an impressive corporate client roster including Google, Meta, Netflix, Bumble, Pinterest, Lululemon, and Tom Ford. The business holds perfect 5-star ratings across Yelp (11 reviews), Google, and Thumbtack, and Carly has been nominated as Austin's Best Tarot Reader by the Austin Chronicle every year since 2017. Operating within the $2.3 billion U.S. psychic services industry (growing at 5.5% CAGR), Sound Sight Tarot is well-positioned in Austin's young, affluent, event-driven market -- a metro of 2.6 million residents with a median age of 34.5 and 40.2 million annual downtown visitors. The company's psychology-forward, secular approach and established corporate event pipeline differentiate it from traditional tarot practitioners in an increasingly mainstream wellness category.

Texas Regulatory Context

TX Occupations Code §2155.073 requires psychic service providers to include written disclosures stating: (1) services are for entertainment purposes only, (2) results are not guaranteed, (3) services are not a substitute for professional advice. Failure to comply is a Class A misdemeanor. Sound Sight Tarot's secular, psychology-forward positioning and explicit "not fortune-telling" framing aligns well with this requirement.
