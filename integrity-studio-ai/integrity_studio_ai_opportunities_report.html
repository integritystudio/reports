4. Certification & Licensing

SOC 2 Type II Certification
High Priority 3–6 Month

Integrity Studio claims SOC 2 Type II on its website but it has not been independently verified. If not yet completed, this is the highest-priority certification for enterprise SaaS sales.

Why It Matters
Enterprise procurement teams require SOC 2 Type II. Without a verifiable report, enterprise deals stall in security review.
Cost
$20,000–$35,000 first year (startup scope); $3,000–$10,000/year compliance platform (Vanta, Drata, Sprinto); 100–300 hours staff time
Action
If completed: make audit report available via NDA to prospects. If not: engage auditor (e.g., Bright Defense, Thoropass). Use Vanta or Sprinto for automation. Timeline: 3–6 months for Type II observation window.
ISO 27001 Certification
Medium Priority 6–12 Month

International standard for information security management. Increasingly required by EU enterprises alongside SOC 2.

Why It Matters
EU enterprises often require ISO 27001 over SOC 2. Dual certification (SOC 2 + ISO 27001) covers both US and EU buyers.
Cost
$6,000–$15,000 for small business (1–25 employees); $15,000–$35,000 ongoing
Action
Pursue after SOC 2 completion (shared controls reduce effort). Use same compliance platform. Consider Scytale or Vanta for combined SOC 2 + ISO 27001.

6. Industry Trends & Technology
