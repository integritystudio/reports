# Austin Metro & Texas Market Analysis Research Brief
**Date:** 2026-03-18
**Purpose:** Market data for creative/marketing/branding agency operating in Austin, TX

---

## Executive Summary

1. The Austin-Round Rock MSA reached **2.55 million residents** as of mid-2025, ranked 25th largest US metro, with consistent growth averaging **1.7% annually** — outpacing all major Texas metros.
2. The metro economy supports **nearly 1 million employed workers** across 79,580 regional businesses, with a **median household income of $100,431** — well above state and national medians.
3. Austin's tech sector employs **203,000 workers** (16.2% of workforce) and the creative/digital media industry supports **50,000–70,000+ jobs**, providing a large, high-income client base for agency services.
4. Texas leads all US states in net domestic migration and business formation, with nearly **500,000 new business applications** in 2024 — creating ongoing demand for brand and marketing services.
5. Austin is ranked **#1 best city to start a business** (USA Today, 2025) and **#5 North American tech talent market** (CBRE, 2024), positioning it as a premier market for agency growth.

## Open Questions & Gaps

- **Austin-specific ad agency revenue:** No published market-size figure exists for Austin alone. Closest proxy: Texas advertising-related services at ~$988M (Statista, 2024).
- **Exact employer establishment count (metro):** The Texas Comptroller 10-county Capital Region figure (79,580, 2022) is the best aggregate available. For current-year Travis County data, direct query of Census County Business Patterns at [data.census.gov](https://data.census.gov) is recommended.
- **Creative industry employment (current-year):** City of Austin and Opportunity Austin figures (50,000–70,000) appear to be rolling estimates without a clear ACS or BLS source year. The BLS does not publish a standalone "creative economy" category.
- **Austin advertising spend per capita or total:** Not published in public sources; would require IBISWorld or BIA/Kelsey paid reports.
