## Executive Summary

Kollective Group is a boutique executive search and recruitment firm headquartered in San Antonio, TX (Travis Park Plaza, 711 Navarro St, 78205), specializing in Sales, Marketing, and Technology placements. The firm offers three service models — RPO, retained search, and contingency search — targeting high-growth retail, e-commerce, and technology companies. Kollective Group is a small operation (2–11 employees per third-party sources; 11–50 per LinkedIn) with one confirmed team member: Nicki (Rumanek) Geary, based in Austin, TX. The firm claims over a decade of recruitment experience but discloses no founding year, named founders, client logos, or case studies. No third-party reviews, BBB listing, or Google Business Profile exists. The global executive search market is valued at $63.99B (2026) growing at 10.11% CAGR, while the RPO market is projected to reach $22.9B by 2030 at 15.4% CAGR.
